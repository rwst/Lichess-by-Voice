What data is collected?

Playing on lichess.org ("Lichess") by using the app generates game and usage data on Lichess. Please see the Lichess Privacy Policy.

In addition the app requests and stores a security token for user authentication at and secure (HTTPS) communication with Lichess. You can remove this token from your Android device by uninstalling the app.

What data is not collected?

The app uses the microphone to perform its function, but no audio data is stored or transmitted. Usage of a specific chess grammar by the app ensures that every speech recognition result that is not a chess move is discarded by the app. The relevant source code is in de.lichessbyvoice.chess.ChessGrammar and de.lichessbyvoice.chess.TextFilter.
